
I interviewed the founder of "Pen and Post," a local independent publishing platform for articles and books. 
The target audience consists of avid readers, freelance journalists, and indie authors looking for a home for their long-form writing. 
The client requested a clean, literary-style aesthetic with a cream or white background, dark elegant typography, and clear grid layouts to showcase book covers and recent articles. 
The main action visitors should take is to read the featured articles or browse the book catalog.
